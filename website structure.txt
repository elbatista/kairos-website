---

## **2. Home Page (Landing)**

**Goal:** Capture attention, explain the problem, and highlight KAIROS as the solution.

**Sections:**

1. **Hero Section**

   * Headline: *“Real-Time Reasoning for Autonomous AI over Evolving Knowledge”*
   * Subheadline: *“KAIROS enables enterprises to deploy AI agents that reason incrementally over graph-based knowledge at scale.”*
   * CTA: “Request a Demo” or “Learn More”
   * Visual: Animated diagram showing AI agent interacting with a dynamic knowledge graph.

2. **Problem Statement**

   * Short bullets or icons:

     * LLMs lack access to evolving enterprise data
     * Knowledge Graphs struggle with dynamic, incremental queries
     * Existing solutions are computationally heavy

3. **Solution / Value Proposition**

   * Highlight 3–4 core capabilities:

     * Incremental reasoning over evolving graphs
     * Distributed, fault-tolerant, cloud-neutral architecture
     * Multi-step, agentic AI integration
     * High performance and scalability
   * Include small graphic or illustration for each feature

4. **Why KAIROS**

   * Short, punchy points: “Cut computational overhead by only processing what changes”, “Enable autonomous agents to act on real-time knowledge”, “Cloud-neutral deployment across any infrastructure”

5. **CTA Section**

   * “Schedule a Demo” or “Contact Us”

## **4. Technology / How It Works**

**Goal:** Explain the architecture and innovative aspects.

**Sections:**

1. **Architecture Overview**

   * Diagram showing distributed KG storage, incremental computation, and AI agent integration
   * Highlight cloud-neutral, on-prem support

2. **Key Features / Innovations**

   * Incremental reasoning paradigm
   * Distributed, strongly consistent replication
   * Middleware layer connecting structured knowledge to AI
   * Reuse of intermediate states / caching
   * Performance highlights: low-latency, scalable, fault-tolerant

3. **Technical Specs / Integrations**

   * Compatible connectors: Kafka, SQL, future extensibility
   * Supported deployment options: cloud or on-prem
   * Performance metrics from benchmarks (optional: animated charts)
